## Vulnerabilities Found and Fixed

### Level 211: Name + Year Pattern (john1980)
- **Type**: Common name with year
- **Issue**: Password 'john1980' was accepted
- **Fix**: Added name+year patterns to blacklist
- **Status**: ✅ FIXED

### Level 268-275: Special Characters with Low Unique Chars
- pass`word, pass$word, pass&word, etc.
- **Type**: Common word with special char
- **Fix**: Added unique character requirement (6 for short passwords)
- **Status**: ✅ FIXED
